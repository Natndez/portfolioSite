Get to know me

Why Coding

Programming, for me, is a way to bring my imagination to life. It allows you to have a vision, and bring that vision into reality. You can literally create anything you can imagine. All that it takes is a little bit of patience and dedication, and you can walk away with something truly amazing. It reminds me of playing with legos as a kid. You start off by putting two tiny squares together, and before you know it, you've built a castle. Coding is my way to find beauty in the little things, and to bring those little things together to create something huge! I've never found a hobby that challenges me as much as programming, but that is what makes it so rewarding!

My Journey

I grew up in a Cuban-American household in a small town in the midwest. In school, I always enjoyed solving puzzles. Especially those that required logical reasoning. Although I didn't fully understand that, I knew that I deeply enjoyed examining problems that required me to think critically. I didn't discover the allure of programming until the end of my first year of University. From that day, I knew that's what I wanted to do, and I haven't looked back since.
